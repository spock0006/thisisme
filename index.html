Learn About Me

God. Family. Country.

I grew up in a large of family of 12 children. We were mostly raised in a town called Merrimack in the great state of New Hampshire. I love my home and I love my family. Scouting trips, church youth groups, speech & debate, piano performance, Legos, and playing sports with my brothers were all key components of my growing up years. Along the way, I gained a strong and undeniable bond with my Heavenly Father and His Son, Our Savior Jesus Christ. Love for them and my fellow men inspired me to serve a mission and now, at college, continue to serve God in His Restored Church. Alongside a love for God, a love for family and country has always been unquestionable in my life. I owe so much to them, and so I strive to make whatever sacrifices I can.

God, Family, Country. My three great loyalties.
